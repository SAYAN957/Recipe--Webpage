Gulab Jaman

Ingredeants

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • A pinch of baking soda

For Syrup

  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 2–3 cardamom pods

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, soda, ghee, and milk into soft dough.
  2. Make small balls and fry on low heat till golden.
  3. Boil sugar, water, cardamom for 5 mins to make syrup.
  4. Soak fried balls in warm syrup for 1–2 hours.

Sondesh

Ingredeants

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3–4 tbsp powdered sugar
  • Rose water/cardamom (optional)
  • Pistachios for garnish

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle.
  2. Strain and hang chhena for 30 mins.
  3. Mash smooth, mix sugar & flavor.
  4. Cook for 4–5 mins on low flame.
  5. Shape, garnish, and chill before serving.
